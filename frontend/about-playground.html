About Us

Our mission is to help people eat healthier and live better lives. We believe that eating healthy should be easy, delicious, and personalized. We combine the latest in nutrition science with the world's best chefs to create personalized recipe recommendations based on your body mass index (BMI). We are a team of food lovers, nutritionists, and engineers who believe in the power of technology to make our lives better. We are passionate about helping people eat healthier, and we are committed to creating a world where everyone has access to delicious, healthy food.
